Through holistic action, Partners Relief and Development demonstrate God's love to children and communities made vulnerable by war in Burma. Partners Relief & Development is a registered charity in Australia, Canada, New Zealand, Norway, the United Kingdom and the United States, working with communities impacted by war in Burma. We are a community committed to the leadership of Jesus. We follow Christ's teachings. As he modeled a life of love, so we are committed to demonstrating love. We pray and seek God's guidance. We are committed to empower individuals and communities and co-create sustainable solutions to their poverty. We are a voice for the displaced and oppressed. We seek system change.
