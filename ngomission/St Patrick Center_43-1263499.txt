St. Patrick Center provides opportunities for self-sufficiency and dignity to persons who are homeless or at risk of becoming homeless. Individuals achieve permanent, positive changes in their lives through affordable housing, sound mental health, employment and financial stability. St. Patrick Center is the largest provider of homeless services in Missouri, with more than 20 programs assisting more than 9.000 persons annually. We help individuals and families move from homelessness to independence in a measurable, cost-effective manner. Since 1983, St. Patrick Center has helped lead more than 123,000 homeless and poor men, women and their families to lives of self-sufficiency and dignity.
