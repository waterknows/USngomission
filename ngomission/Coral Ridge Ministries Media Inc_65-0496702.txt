Truth in Action Ministries' mission is to equip and encourage Christ-followers to find their God-given, Kingdom assignments - and to enable them to connect their passions and abilities to God's calling on their lives, so that they might impact the world around them. Truth in Action provides: -	Biblical worldview training-	Resources for Christians to discover their gifts and calling-	Training and opportunities to connect believers in communities.-	Interactive Internet tools and video resources to equip,   encourage, and engage the next generation.
