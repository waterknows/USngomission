Founded in 1911, Loyola Marymount University (LMU) is a Catholic university rooted in the Jesuit and Marymount traditions. Our mission is the encouragement of learning, the education of the whole person, and the service of faith and the promotion of justice. LMU offers more than 80 undergraduate degrees and programs. The Graduate Division offers 30 master's degrees, one doctoral degree and 15 credential programs. Our enrollment includes 5,509 undergraduate, 1,962 graduate and 1,374 law school students. Our 150-acre bluff-top campus is located in West Los Angeles and was recently named in the 10 Most Beautiful Campuses by the Princeton Review.
