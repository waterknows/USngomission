ICAN's mission is to provide free, comprehensive programs that empower youth to be productive, self-confident, and responsible members of the community. ICAN serves 1,300 youth and their families living predominantly in the Downtown Chandler community, where daily challenges like substance abuse, gang activity, and violence are considered the norm. There are over 10,000 youth living in this area, with needs far greater than elsewhere in the Valley. Of 10 youth living in this area:-4 are living in single parent households-8 are living in extreme poverty, in households earning less than $25,000 annually for a family of four on average-4 will be using drugs or alcohol to cope with the harsh realities of their community -5 will be experiencing some form of academic failure-2 will not graduate high school by the age of 25
