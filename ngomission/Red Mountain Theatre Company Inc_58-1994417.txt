Founded in 1992, Red Mountain Theater Company (RMTC) is a non-profit professional theatre organization in Birmingham, Alabama with an emphasis on musical theatre. RMTC provides unique educational, training and performing opportunities for aspiring and culturally diverse artists, while growing new and existing audiences through quality theatrical experiences. RMTC offers youth programs that educate, train and polish the performing arts skills of youth ranging for ages 9-18 and offers area youth the chance to perform throughout the state.
