WHO IS PET PARTNERS?Pet Partners is the nation's largest and most prestigious nonprofit registering handlers of multiple species as volunteer teams providing Animal-Assisted Interactions.  With the highest caliber curriculum in the industry, Pet Partners trains volunteers and evaluates them with their pets for visiting animal programs in hospitals, nursing homes, veterans' centers, hospice, Alzheimer's facilities, courtrooms, schools and other settings. MORE THAN DOGSPet Partners understands that different species of animals are therapeutic to different people so we proudly register nine appropriate species including: cats, horses, birds, pigs, rabbits, llamas & alpacas, guinea pigs and even rats for therapy visits. MAKING A BIG IMPACTLast year, Pet Partners teams made more than one million visits in all 50 states to people in need.
