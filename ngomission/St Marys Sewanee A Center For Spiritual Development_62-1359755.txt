St. Mary's Sewanee: The Ayres Center for Spiritual Development is dedicated to providing spiritual hospitality to persons of diverse religious and spiritual backgrounds, in a place of natural beauty, simplicity, silence, tranquility and warm spiritual rest through retreats, renewal and learning. During our 25-year history, St. Mary's Sewanee: The Ayres Center for Spiritual Development has built numerous programs to meet the needs of individuals and groups looking for rest, renewal and reconnection. Whether your needs call for a personal retreat between you and God or building spiritual unity among a group of believers, St. Mary's Sewanee can build the right program for you.
