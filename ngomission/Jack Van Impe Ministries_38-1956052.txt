Jack Van Impe Ministries International seeks to spread the Gospel of Jesus Christ (I Corinthians 15:3,4), the message of His soon return (Titus 2:13), and the truth of the Bible (II Timothy 3:16) to all peoples in all nations (Acts 1:8) using all available resources to bring Him honor and glory (Colossians 1:18). Jack Van Impe Ministries ministers through soul-winning outreaches: global TV and radio, Internet, End-time movies, print publications, audiocassettes, biblical counseling correspondence, and biblical material distribution programs.
