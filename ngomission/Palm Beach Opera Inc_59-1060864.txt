Palm Beach Opera (PBO) is dedicated to producing live opera at an international standard of excellence and to enriching the life of the communities it serves with a diverse offering of educational programs. Founded in 1961 as the Civic Opera of the Palm Beaches, the fully professional Palm Beach Opera presents main stage performances from January through March at the Kravis Center for the Performing Arts in West Palm Beach. Palm Beach Opera is committed to arts education and offers a variety of educational programs for all ages and levels. In addition to the fully staged live opera performances, PBO offers an operatic Lunch & Learn series, One Opera in One Hour series, and maintains an active Young Artists Program.
