United Way Silicon Valley Mission: To focus community resources to help families and individuals fully engage in increasing their self-sufficiency, educational achievement and opportunity for success.Our Vision: We envision a caring, engaged and vibrant community where families and individuals achieve their full potential through education, health and financial stability.Our Strategic Focus: We help families raising children from birth through middle school become financially secure, healthy and more connected to their community so they are better able to support their children's educational success.
