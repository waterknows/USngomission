RiverQuest is a non-profit educational organization that operates a river learning center for students, teachers and the community of Southwestern Pennsylvania. Pittsburgh Voyager, Inc. was founded in 1991. The agency began working with students in 1995, and in 2006 began working under the name RiverQuest. RiverQuest provides exceptional river-based educational adventure programs for students, teachers and community. Programs utilize hands-on approaches and technology to further the development of critical thinking skills, technological literacy and environmental awareness. The mission of RiverQuest is to engage students and people of all ages in hands-on science learning experiences that develop critical thinking skills and promote awareness of our river ecosystems.
