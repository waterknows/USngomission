Wesleyan School was established in 1963 as an integral part of Sandy Springs United Methodist Church. Wesleyan's mission is to be a Christian school of academic excellence by providing each student a diverse college preparatory education guided by Christian principles and beliefs; by challenging and nurturing the mind, body, and spirit; and by developing responsible stewardship in our changing world. Enrollment is 1,083 students in kindergarten through grade 12 for the 2008-09 academic year. The Class of 2008 graduated 106 students, bringing our alumni population to 843.
