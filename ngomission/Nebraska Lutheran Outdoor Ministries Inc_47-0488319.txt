Nebraska Lutheran Outdoor Ministries (NLOM) is the camping, conferencing and retreating ministry of the Nebraska Synod, Evangelical Lutheran Church in America. NLOM is called to provide places set apart for people of all ages to grow in Christ. Founded in 1975, NLOM encourages spiritual renewal for the whole person by providing encounters with the Word, nurturing an awareness of creation as God's gift, and offering opportunities for worship, recreation, and the development of relationships. NLOM serves groups and individuals with program goals of Christian growth and education, to be accomplished through camping, conferencing and retreating.
