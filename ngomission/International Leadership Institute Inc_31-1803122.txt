The International Leadership Institute (ILI) changes history by accelerating the spread of the life-transforming power of the Gospel through leaders of leaders empowered by the Holy Spirit. ILI trains and mobilizes more than 23,000 leaders each year with the eight core values of the most effective Christian leaders. ILI alumni serve on the front lines in nearly every sector of society. They live in more than 108 nations and speak more than 200 languages. They are highly committed to impact their communities and nations through equipping leaders to spread the Gospel.
