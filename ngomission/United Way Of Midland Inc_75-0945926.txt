United Way of Midland supports programs and services that target community needs by focusing on the building blocks for a better life: a quality education that leads to stable employment; financial independence and sufficient housing to support a family, and sound emotional and physical health. United Way of Midland connects the dots between our community's issues and real solutions all made possible with the gifts of time, talent, and financial resources. To LIVE UNITED means being a part of the change, and your participation is crucial to our success.
