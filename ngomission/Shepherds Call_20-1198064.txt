The Hope Line (Dawson McAllister Association) communicates biblical truths of Christianity to teens and young adults. Dawson McAllister is recognized internationally as a gifted youth communicator. He can now be heard on over 60 Top 40 mainstream stations in the U.S., plus can be heard all over the world via three internet streams available 24/7. The ministry augments its radio and internet outreach through TheHopeLine, a 1-800 number that is manned 24/7 with trained HopeCoaches. HopeCoaches have handled hundreds of thousands of calls and chats from desperate and hurting teenagers and young adults whose issues and concerns are complex and deep. In 2013, TheHopeLine intervened in over 3,000 suicide-related incidents.
