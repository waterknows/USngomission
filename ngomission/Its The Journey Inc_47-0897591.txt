It's The Journey, Inc.'s mission is to strengthen Georgia's breast cancer community by raising money and awareness for local organizations that focus on breast cancer education, early detection, awareness and support services, as well as the unmet needs in the breast cancer community. It's The Journey, Inc. - producer of the Atlanta 2-Day Walk for Breast Cancer- is a local organization founded 10 years ago by a breast cancer survivor, Randi Passoff. The purpose of the 2-Day Walk is to raise funds for breast cancer programs throughout Georgia.
