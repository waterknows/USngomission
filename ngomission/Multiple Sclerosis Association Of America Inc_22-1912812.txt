Founded in 1970, the Multiple Sclerosis Association of America (MSAA) is a national 501(c)(3) charitable organization dedicated to being a leading resource for the entire MS community, improving lives today through vital services and support. MSAA provides free direct services to MS patients that improve their quality of life and enable them to continue living rich and independent lives. We are dedicated to serving our mission through client services and symptom management research; and to promoting, expanding, and encouraging public knowledge and awareness and support as to the day-to-day concerns of those with MS.MSAA offers a variety of programs and services in all 50 states: Helpline consultations, equipment distribution, cooling therapy, MRI diagnostic funding, resource assistance, and public awareness campaigns. MSAA also provides valuable information on the disease and symptom management through its award winning literature and educational videos.
