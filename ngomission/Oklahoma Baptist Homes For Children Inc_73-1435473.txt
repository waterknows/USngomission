Since 1903, Oklahoma Baptist Homes for Children (OBHC) has provided hope and homes for children affected by abuse, abandonment, neglect, or poverty. OBHC is the largest provider of private, not-for-profit, residential childcare in the state. Our mission is to help children become capable, caring Christian adults by sharing Christ's love and providing hope and homes for children. OBHC provides and maintains childcare facilities for dependent, neglected, and/or needy children of Oklahoma in order to meet their spiritual, physical, mental and emotional needs.
