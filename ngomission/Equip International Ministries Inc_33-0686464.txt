Founded in 1996, EQUIP is dedicated to developing servant leaders who shoulder responsibility, meet challenges with courage and creativity and never relinquish their God-given dreams. EQUIP's mission is to equip international Christian leaders to effectively serve the growing Body of Christ around the world. We provide strategic leadership training through conferences, resources, partnerships and technology. EQUIP provides leadership development resources to pastor and leaders in 118 nations. They teach practical topics, such as vision casting, priorities, team building, conflict management, communication and relational skills, mentoring, and personal growth. EQUIP provides books and other printed materials, video, DVD and audio-based training to leaders around the world.
