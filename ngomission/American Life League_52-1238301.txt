Founded in 1979, American Life League (ALL) is an educational organization committed to the protection of all innocent human beings from fertilization to death. ALL works in a variety of areas, including grassroots activities, public policy, youth outreach, communications, development, and resources. ALL includes such projects as Celebrate Life magazine, Rock for Life, STOPP International and the Crusade for the Defense of Our Catholic Church, and also coordinates a network of more than 60 local and regional pro-life groups across the nation.
