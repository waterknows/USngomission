North Yarmouth Academy (NYA) is an independent, college preparatory, coeducational day school serving students in grades six through twelve. The school offers a structured program that sets clear standards and high expectations in an environment that emphasizes values of mutual respect, trust and community. The NYA program encourages students to develop: problem-solving skills; sophistication in writing, speaking and artistic expression; appreciation for the importance of athletic activity, teamwork and cooperation; self-confidence and self-respect through a breadth of experiences; concern for the larger community and an appreciation for the natural environment; and desire to continue the process of lifelong learning.
