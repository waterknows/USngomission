The Child Development Association (CDA) provides affordable early learning programs and childcare for preschool children of low-income families in the metro Atlanta area. The CDA prepares economically disadvantaged children for success in education and life. And, our childcare services enable hardworking parents to maintain full-time employment to support their families.



The CDA is a vibrant organization that has provided outstanding service to low-income, hard-working families and their young children for 45 years.
