Founded in 1954, the National Multiple Sclerosis Society, Utah-Southern Idaho Chapter is the local extension of the National Multiple Sclerosis Society. The Utah State Chapter was incorporated on December 6, 1954 as the local extension of the National Multiple Sclerosis Society. On October 1, 2010 the chapter expanded to include Southern Idaho, creating the Utah-Southern Idaho Chapter. Headquartered in Salt Lake City with a field office in Boise, Idaho, the Chapter provides a wide variety of programs, fundraisers, activities, and services throughout the Chapter region to people with MS, their families, and friends.
