Since 1894, the Nantucket Historical Association (NHA) has worked to preserve and interpret the history of Nantucket Island in order to foster an appreciation of its historic significance. The NHA Research Library is a special collections repository of primary resources focusing on Nantucket history; these collections contain more than 5,000 volumes and 50,000 photographs, as well as archival documents such as ship's logs, account books, family papers, and scrapbooks.
