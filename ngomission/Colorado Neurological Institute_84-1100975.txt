The mission of the Colorado Neurological Institute (CNI) is to enhance the delivery of personalized, comprehensive and state-of-the-art care to patients with neurological conditions through coordinated patient care, education, research and outreach activities. Founded in 1988, CNI is today the largest and most comprehensive neuroscience center in the Rocky Mountain region. Through our distinguished physician membership, we treat a full spectrum of neurological disorders, including epilepsy, brain tumors, traumatic brain injury, hearing disorders, movement disorders, stroke, sleep disorders, migraines, neuromuscular disorders, and more. CNI also partners with other leading medical organizations to enhance the breadth and depth of capabilities available to patients and their families.
