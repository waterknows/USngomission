Founded in 1030, the Goodwill of Southern Indiana's mission is to serve children, adults and their community through the power of donated good. They are leading the community in providing early childhood development, family support and adult career achievement through program collaborations and successful Goodwill stores. Services provided by the Goodwill of Southern Indiana include, employment resources, the Children's Learning Center (formally the Children's Academy), Goodwill of Southern Indiana stores, family support services, recycling efforts and a voucher program.
