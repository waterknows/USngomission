Founded in 1953, WPBT/Channel 2 was Florida's first noncommercial television station. Since its beginnings, Channel 2 has developed and maintained a technologic and programmatic superiority for which it is recognized and respected throughout the industry. It continues to demonstrate its worth to the South Florida region by providing quality content to educate, enlighten, inspire and entertain. Today, with a signal reach from the Treasure Coast to Key West, Channel 2 attracts an audience of over one million households each week, and consistently ranks as one of the 10 most-watched public television stations in the country.
