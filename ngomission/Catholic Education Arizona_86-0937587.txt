Catholic Education Arizona (CEA) exists solely to help deserving students attend Catholic schools and better their lives. Through these endeavors and your tax credit redirect dollars, we are all transforming culture. Catholic Education Arizona is the biggest provider of scholarship funds to private schools. Founded in 1998, CEA provides scholarships to students at 40 Phoenix Diocese schools (K-12) through tax credits. Without spending a penny, donors can join 14,000 others who channel more than $12 million each year to local students.
