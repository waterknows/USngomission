Founded in 1907, Berkshire School provides young men and women with the intellectual and moral foundations and the leadership skills that will enable them to flourish as members of a family and a community as they confront the challenges of college and beyond. Berkshire's enrollment of 382 consists of both day and boarding students from 24 different countries. The Berkshire Model encompasses such programs as the Advanced Math/Science Research program, the Center for Sustainability, Pro Vita Winter Session, and the Center for Global Learning. These programs have been defining ones over the last few years and will continue to define the School for years to come.
