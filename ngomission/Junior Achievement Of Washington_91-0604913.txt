Junior Achievement of Washington provides a series of business, economics, and life skills programs to enhance the education of young people. Junior Achievement of Washington was established in 1953 and reaches more than 100,000 kindergarten through twelfth-grade students per year. Junior Achievement maintains an active vision, front and center, on how we can have a positive impact on the lives of more students - guided by our core values: commitment to the principles of market-based economics and entrepreneurship; passion for what we do and honesty, integrity, and excellence in how we do it; and conviction in the educational and motivational impact of relevant, hands-on learning.
