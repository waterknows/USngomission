The Mobius Spokane Children's Museum and Science Center has worked toward the goal of bringing a science center to the Inland Northwest for more than a decade. Mobius Children's Museum is a fun, nurturing place for young children and families to learn and grow through hands-on activities in science, culture and the arts. Mobius Science Center is an engaging, hands-on, informal learning environment. Our science shows are an exciting way to demonstrate various principles of chemistry and the physical sciences
