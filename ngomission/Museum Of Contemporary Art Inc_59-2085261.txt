Founded in 1981, the Museum of Contemporary Art (MOCA) is dedicated to making contemporary art accessible to diverse audiences - especially underserved populations - through the collection, preservation and exhibition of the best of contemporary art and its art historical influences. The Museum of Contemporary Art is known for its provocative and innovative exhibitions, and for seeking a fresh approach in examining the art of our time. The museum maintains an active exhibition schedule, presenting 8 to 10 exhibitions annually.
