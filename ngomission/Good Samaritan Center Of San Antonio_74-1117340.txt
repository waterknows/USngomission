Established in 1951, Good Samaritan Community Services (GSCS) works to serve as a catalyst for change, supporting individuals and families by providing excellent community services to overcome the impact of poverty. GSCS provides comprehensive services to more than 5,000 individuals across seven (7) locations around South Texas. GSCS actively serves as a place of change, reaching out to support thousands of people working to improve their lives and to overcome the challenges of poverty.
