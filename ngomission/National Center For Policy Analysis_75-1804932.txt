Established in 1983, the NCPA is a nonprofit, nonpartisan think tank located in Dallas, Texas. The mission of the NCPA is to promote innovative, free market solutions to public policy problems. The NCPA pursues its mission by identifying, encouraging, and communicating scholarly research, focusing particularly upon the issues of health care, taxes and the economy, retirement, energy and the environment, and education . The NCPA communicates daily with policymakers, opinion leaders, and the general public, advising them about the policy challenges our country faces and recommending innovative policy solutions.
