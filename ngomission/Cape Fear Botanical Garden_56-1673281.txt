Founded in 1989, Cape Fear Botanical Garden now boasts more than 2,000 varieties of ornamental plants and has several specialty gardens, including Camellia, Daylily and Hosta gardens. Cape Fear Botanical Garden serves this region as a center for: enrichment, inspiration and enjoyment of nature; the collection, culture and aesthetic display of plants; encouragement of environmental stewardship; conservation, education and research; the preservation of our agricultural heritage; and engagement and involvement of the community.
