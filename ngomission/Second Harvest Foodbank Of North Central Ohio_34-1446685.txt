Second Harvest Food Bank of North Central Ohio, founded in 1982, is the largest regional hunger-relief organization with a network of over 100 partner charities serving Crawford, Erie, Huron and Lorain Counties. Second Harvest will secure and distribute an estimated 7.5 million pounds of donated food and grocery products this year; and support approximately 150 programs including food pantries, soup kitchens, emergency shelters, after-school programs, BackPack Programs and Kids Cafes. For every $1 donated we are able to distribute enough food for 5 nutritious meals.
