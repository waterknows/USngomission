Founded in 1974, The United Way of Santa Cruz County's mission is to mobilize our county's residents to create opportunities for a good life for all. United Way of Santa Cruz County focuses on three community areas - Youth Success, Family Financial Stability, and Health. If you care about helping youth succeed in school, helping people attain financial stability and/or want to make sure families live healthy and responsible lives, an investment in United Way will a difference in people's lives.
