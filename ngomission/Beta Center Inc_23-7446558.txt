BETA Center's mission is to provide children and parents with the knowledge and support needed for strong and healthy families. BETA Center has 32 years of experience helping families, regardless of age or economic status, in the greater Orlando area including Orange, Osceola and Seminole counties. With a focus on parenting, teen moms and at-risk families, BETA Center changes behaviors and lives. The agency offers a wide range of services to support children and families, increase healthy family functioning and prevent child abuse and neglect. We reach more than 17,000 people annually through the areas of Parenting Education Services, Pregnant/Parenting Teen Program, Neighborhood-based Family Services, Emergency Family Support Services and the Residential Program.
