Founded in 1869, Rye Country Day School is a coeducational, college preparatory school dedicated to providing students from pre-kindergarten through grade 12 with an excellent education using both traditional and innovative approaches. In a nurturing and supportive environment, we offer a challenging program that stimulates individuals to achieve their maximum potential through academic, athletic, creative and social endeavors. We are actively committed to diversity, expect and promote moral responsibility, and strive to develop strength of character within a respectful school community. Our goal is to foster a lifelong passion for knowledge, understanding and service in an ever-changing world.
