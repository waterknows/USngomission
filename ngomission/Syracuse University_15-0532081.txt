Officially chartered in 1870 as a private, coeducational institution, Syracuse University (SU) promotes learning through teaching, research, scholarship, creative accomplishment, and service. Syracuse's vision is to be a university whose five core values of quality, caring, diversity, innovation, and service are pervasive and evident among all its members. SU is a diverse institution of higher education dedicated to faculty excellence and scholarly distinction, attracting and supporting enterprising students and a close interaction and engagement with the world - locally, nationally and globally. Syracuse has an estimated enrollment of over 19,000 undergraduate and graduate students and 909 full time faculty.
