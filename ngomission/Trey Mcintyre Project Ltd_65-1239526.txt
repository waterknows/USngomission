The Trey McIntyre Project (TMP) is a Boise-based organization founded to nurture, support and produce the artistic vision of artist Trey McIntyre. TMP was founded by Trey McIntyre, John Michael Schert, and Anne Mueller in 2004 and became a full-time, year-round company headquartered in Boise, ID in early 2008. TMP believes in the power of art and dance to transform, heal and enlighten.
