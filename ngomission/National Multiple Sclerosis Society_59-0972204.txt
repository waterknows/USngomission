The National Multiple Sclerosis Society, Mid Florida Chapter is a community of individuals who are committed to achieving a world free of MS. Founded in 1958, the Mid Florida Chapter serves more than 70,000 people in a 23 county area, including family members, friends, caregivers, and employers. There are an estimated 10,000 people living with MS in Mid Florida. We provide comprehensive support services, referrals, literature, self help groups, advocacy, and educational programs to people with MS, their family and friends, and raise funds locally to support the National MS Society's research initiatives. The Mid Florida Chapter is proud to be a source of empowerment for those living with MS.
