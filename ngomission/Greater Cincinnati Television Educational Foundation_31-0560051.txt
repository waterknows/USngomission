CET is a leading provider of education and enrichment in classrooms and living rooms throughout greater Cincinnati. Through PBS and local programming, innovative multimedia curriculum projects, parent workshops and teacher training, CET positively impacts millions of lives in our community. For more than 50 years, CET has been bringing greater Cincinnati television that stimulates the imagination, challenges the intellect, encourages achievement and celebrates diversity. CET's mission is to use its resources to educate, enrich and engage the communities it serves.
