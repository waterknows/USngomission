For more than 60 years, United Way of Clallam County has been advancing the common good in Clallam County by investing in the building blocks of a good life - Education, Income and Health. United Way of Clallam County's mission is to strengthen our community by helping people to live safe, healthy and productive lives. We do this in a variety of ways, working with the community to provide leadership in meeting human service needs, including raising and distributing local donations, and being a constructive voice for human service needs in Clallam County.
