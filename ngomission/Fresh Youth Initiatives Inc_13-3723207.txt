FYI is a non-profit youth development organization in NYC providing young people ages 9-18 with the support, encouragement, and skills they need to become productive, confident, and caring young adults. We keep young people safe and engaged afterschool and in the summer using our Four Pillars of Success:-	Community service activities-	Exposure to new and positive experiences-	Academic support-	Personal guidance  Our mission is to engage youth in positive, community-building activities while guiding and supporting them through adolescence. Our vision is that all young people develop their potential; become meaningful contributors in their communities; and lead fulfilled, productive lives.
