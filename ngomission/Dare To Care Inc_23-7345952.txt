Founded in 1971, Dare to Care Food Bank works to end hunger in our community through partnerships, innovative programs, and community engagement. We serve serves 13 counties including: Jefferson, Shelby, Bullitt, Oldham, Spencer, Carroll, Trimble, and Henry in Kentucky, and Floyd, Clark, Harrison, Washington, and Crawford in Indiana. Our 55,000 square foot warehouse is equipped to store and inventory food at the highest commercial food safety standards. In the past year, Dare to Care distributed food for 13 million meals to local families struggling with hunger.
