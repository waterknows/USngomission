One Israel Fund (OIF) is dedicated to supporting the welfare and safety of the Jewish people of Judea and Samaria as well as rebuilding the lives of those impacted by the Gaza evacuation. These nearly 400,000 citizens of Israel are the vanguard of Israel's security and sovereignty as a Jewish State. Working with the communities, government. officials and the IDF, OIF fills the gaps in medical, social, recreational and preventive security services which are conspicuously lacking. Its goal is to undertake ongoing fundraising campaigns to help ensure the physical, emotional and moral well-being of the Jewish people living in every community in these areas. Providing a broad spectrum of communal needs, One Israel Fund is the premier North American charity whose efforts are dedicated to the Jewish areas in these areas.
