Founded in 1991, the Spokane Valley Partners is the sole provider of comprehensive social services in the Greater Spokane Valley, encompassing everything east of the Spokane City limits to the Idaho border. The mission of Spokane Valley Partners is to improve quality of life for those in need by advocating self-reliance through partnerships and education while providing basic life necessities. Spokane Valley Partners serves low-income individuals and families, and those undergoing hardships such as illness, job loss, or death of a family member, disabilities and other life-altering events. Spokane Valley Partners provides food, clothing, emergency services, information, education, and assistance in achieving long-term self-reliance. We also help clients access other services available to them in our community.
