American Hiking Society is the only national non-profit organization dedicated to promoting and protecting the trails you love to hike. It promotes and protects America's hiking trails, their surrounding natural areas, and the hiking experience.In just this last year alone American Hiking Society has: worked to prevent off-road vehicles from using hikers-only trails in cooperation with the Forest Service; sent more than 500 volunteers to parks and public lands to build and maintain hiking trails, and protected millions of acres of prime hiking locations through the passage of federal legislation. With your support, American Hiking will continue to ensure that the places you love to hike will be protected for you and for future generations.
