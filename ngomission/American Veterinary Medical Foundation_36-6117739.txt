The American Veterinary Medical Foundation (AVMF) is the charitable arm of the AVMA. For over 50 years, AVMF has been dedicated to embracing and advancing the well-being and medical care of animals. Charitable contributions and support to the Foundation help veterinarians help animals. Initiatives include: Humane Outreach-Animal Welfare, Education and Public Awareness, Research Support, Student Enhancement and Support of the AVMA and Its Initiatives. In the last decade AVMF has awarded grants totaling nearly $10,000,000.
