Excellent Education for Everyone (E3) provides a 360Â° comprehensive approach to urban educational reform and choice as a technical resource for best practice on policy implementation, a mobilizer of community alliances a catalyst for empowering urban parents, and a legal resource for those most affected by the continued failure of the existing urban public education system.
