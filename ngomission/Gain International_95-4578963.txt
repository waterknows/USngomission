Global Aid Network (GAIN) is a multi-national network of ministries serving to demonstrate the love of God, through word and deed, to hurting and needy people around the world through relief and development projects. As the humanitarian aid arm of Campus Crusade for Christ International, the ministry of GAIN spans the globe helping to meet physical needs in areas of poverty and despair and providing hope through evangelistic outreach. GAIN partners with existing Campus Crusade for Christ ministries, local churches, missionaries, and other agencies to help distribute compassionate aid to impoverished countries around the world. GAIN oversees projects in more than 30 countries having distributed greater than $125 million worth of aid and mobilized more than 10,000 short-term mission participants to foreign lands.
