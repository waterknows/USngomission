El Paso Community Foundation was established in 1977 as a permanent endowment for the long term benefit of far West Texas, southern New Mexico and northern Chihuahua, Mexico. It serves as a charitable resource to donors, nonprofit organizations and the community at-large. The mission of the El Paso Community Foundation is to establish permanent charitable endowments; provide a vehicle for donors' varied interests; promote local philanthropy; and to provide leadership and resources in addressing local challenges and opportunities.
