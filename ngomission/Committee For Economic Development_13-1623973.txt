The Committee for Economic Development (CED) is an independent, nonpartisan organization of senior corporate executives and university leaders dedicated to policy research on the major economic and social issues of our time and the implementation of its recommendations by the public and private sectors. Since its inception in 1942, CED has addressed national priorities that promote sustained economic growth and development to benefit all Americans. CEO is the go-to organization for business engagement in public policy, and is common ground for some of the best minds in business and academia - from the United States and throughout the world. We are a catalyst for community action and are a respected business voice on national and international issues.
