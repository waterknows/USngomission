Established in 1978, Gulfcoast Legal Services is a regional organization dedicated to providing energetic, comprehensive, direct legal advocacy, counseling and education for vulnerable and/or low income individuals in Florida's Pinellas, Sarasota, Hillborough and Manatee Counties. Gulfcoast Legal Services provides legal services for persons who face the deprivation of critical human needs, such as food, shelter, income, health care, family stability or personal safety. Gulfcoast Legal Services provides leadership, public advocacy on behalf of clients and the community.
