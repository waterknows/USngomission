Boy Scouts of America, Indian Waters Council, provides quality youth programs designed to strengthen character, develop good citizenship, and promote physical fitness. The Boy Scouts of America is to prepare young people to make ethical choices over their lifetimes by instilling in them the values of the Scout Oath and Law. The Indian Waters Council provides scouting programs in the Midlands of South Carolina. There's a program for every boy and young man from age 6 to 20 and for every young woman from age 14 to 20.
