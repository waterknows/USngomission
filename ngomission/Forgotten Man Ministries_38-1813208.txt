Established in 1966, Forgotten Man Ministries (FMM) brings together inmates, chaplains, and lay ministers in the study and ministry of God's Word in Michigan county jails.FMM chaplains and associate chaplains conduct daily in-jail ministries that include:* Daily prayer * Bible distribution * Biblical counseling & discipleship * Bible self-study courses * Group Bible studies * Facilitate local church worship services * Teach Biblically based life principles * Maintain the jail library & book cartsJesus told us that we were to go and make disciples of all the nations. This of course includes jail inmates. Today, Forgotten Man Ministries follows in Christ's footsteps by bringing the healing powers of the Great Physician to those incarcerated in our Michigan jails.
