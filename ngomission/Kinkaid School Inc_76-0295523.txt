The oldest independent coeducational school in Houston, The Kinkaid School was founded in 1906 by Margaret Hunter Kinkaid. Kinkaid's mission is to promote educational excellence, personal responsibility, and balanced growth, and thereby to help its students to discover and develop their talents and to fulfill their best potentials. The primary goals of Kinkaid are intellectual endeavor and achievement. The School also believes that a well-rounded education includes experiences in the arts, athletics, and a variety of extra-curricular activities. The Kinkaid School is a non-sectarian college preparatory day school enrolling a total of 1,380 boys and girls in grades prekindergarten through twelve.
