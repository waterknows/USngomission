Incorporated in 1976, the Victoria Theatre Association is one of Dayton's premier arts organization. Our mission is to strengthen the economic and artistic vitality of our community and arts partners through presenting quality and diverse arts experiences and stewardship of our community's state-of-the-art performing venues. We present more than 300 performances for all ages each year, operating and maintaining three vital arts facilities for the benefit of the community and the arts organizations that use them. Victoria Theatre Association manages the Victoria Theatre, the Benjamin and Marian Schuster Performing Arts Center, and the Metropolitan Arts Center, which is home to The Loft Theatre.
