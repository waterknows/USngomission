Founded in 1981, Westside Christian High School strives to equip servant leaders in God's kingdom for the next generation by educating and developing the whole person for the glory of God. Westside Christian High School is a non-denominational high school. The philosophy of the school is distinctly Christian and is based on the Bible. Students are encouraged to be diligent in their studies as well as participate in a wide variety of extra-curricular activities that help them become well-rounded individuals.
