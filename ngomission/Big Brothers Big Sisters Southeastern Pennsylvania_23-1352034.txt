The Big Brothers Big Sisters Southeastern PA Mission is to provide children facing adversity with strong and enduring, professionally supported one-to-one relationships that change their lives for the better, forever. By partnering with parents/guardians, volunteers and others in the community we are accountable for each child in our program achieving: - Higher aspirations, greater confidence, and better relationships - Avoidance of risky behaviors - Educational success The Big Brothers Big Sisters Southeastern PA Vision is that all children achieve success in life.
