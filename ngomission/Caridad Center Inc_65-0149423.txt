Caridad Center's Mission is to upgrade the health, education and living standards for children and families of agricultural workers, laborers and the underserved. Caridad Center's Vision is to eliminate the cycle of poverty for the families that we serve in South Florida. In 2007-2008, the clinics were visited over 24,000 times by patients in both dental and medical. Caridad offers comprehensive services and programs to the whole family: Medical Care (All Specialties), Dental Care, Comprehensive Vision Program, Mental Health Counseling, Chronic Disease Management, Breast Cancer Prevention, After-School Tutoring, College Scholarships, Emergency Assistance, Girl Scout Troops, Back to School Supplies, Holiday Meals and Gifts and Referrals to many other resources in Palm Beach County that can help.
