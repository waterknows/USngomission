Boys Hope Girls Hope, Chapters includes 17 of the nationwide chapters and affiliates of Boys Hope Girls Hope. Founded in 1977 by Father Paul Sheridan, S.J., Boys Hope Girls Hope empowers children to realize the potential that is within them by providing children with arms-around care, safe homes and environments, a first-class education, opportunities to learn more about themselves and the world, and academic, financial, and spiritual support through college. Since 1991, 100% of Boys Hope Girls Hope's U.S. alumni have gone on to college.
