Christian Ministries Foundation is a community foundation that helps individuals, ministries, organizations and foundations make effective gifts to the ministries and charitable agencies or organizations of their choice. Charitable services offered include donor advised funds; designated endowment funds; charitable annuities; charitable remainder unitrusts; charitable lead trusts; charitable trust administration; and revocable living trusts. Christian Ministries Foundation provides stewardship services which promote increased giving by Christian individuals and organizations for the benefit of the Church and its ministries.
