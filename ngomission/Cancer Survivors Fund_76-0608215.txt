Cancer Survivors' Fund (CSF) provides scholarships for young cancer survivors, give them a new purpose and meaning in life and enable them to continue their college education. CSF provides prosthetic limbs to disadvantaged young adults. CSF counsels, provides emotional support, and motivates young cancer survivors and channel their thoughts and dreams to a healthy and productive future. CSF supports young survivors to become volunteers themselves, give others courage and hope to develop a strong willpower to continue their fight with cancer. CSF supports efforts to save lives through cancer research to find more effective treatments and the cure - to win the fight against cancer.
