The mission of Emergency Food Network (EFN) is to provide Pierce County with a consistent, diverse and nutritious food supply so that no person goes hungry.EFN is the largest nonprofit food distributor in Pierce County, providing 70 partner food banks, hot meal sites and shelters with more than 15 million pounds of food each year. The ability to procure donated food, purchase food in bulk and grow food on Mother Earth Farm allows EFN to distribute $12 worth of food for every $1 received.
