The Colorado Center for Nursing Excellence contributes to the promise for health by developing an excellent nursing and health care workforce that meets the needs of all Coloradans. Formed in 2002, the Center is the only neutral, nursing workforce-focused organization operating from a system- and state-wide perspective while at the same time working on the ground with over 175 clinical and educational partners from all segments of Colorado's healthcare workforce pipeline. The Center has used its system-wide perspective and consensus-building credibility to create innovative, high-leverage interventions that help improve the volume, quality and operations of Colorado's nursing workforce.
