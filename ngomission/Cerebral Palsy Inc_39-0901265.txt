Cerebral Palsy, Inc. has been serving Northeast Wisconsin for over 54 years. We strive to promote community involvement, inclusion and enhance the quality of life of all persons. Our skilled staff is here to work with individuals and families by providing support and professional services to those who have been affected by temporary or permanent physical, sensory, and/or speech needs or conditions regardless of what brought on the need for services. Our staff is skilled in dealing with Cerebral Palsy, Muscular Dystrophy, Spina Bifida, Arthritis, Back Pain, Post Stroke, Post Surgical, Chronic Pain, Fibromyalgia, Amputees, Diabetes, Head Injury, Developmental Delays, Accident Recovery, and Sports Injury, just to name a few of the conditions.
