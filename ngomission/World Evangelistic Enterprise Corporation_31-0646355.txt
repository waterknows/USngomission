WEEC (World Evangelistic Enterprise Corporation) works to glorify God by using communications technology with excellence and integrity to lead the lost to Jesus Christ and the Christian into a closer walk with Him. WEEC is a non-denominational Christian radio station that has been broadcasting to southwestern Ohio since December of 1961 at 100.7 and to Marion since May 11th, 2004 at 101.9 on the F.M. dial. We operate 24 hours a day, seven days a week, 365 days a year. Each week, WEEC presents 86 different program titles; 77 hours of uninterrupted music; 168 hours of inspirational and informational programming; and 177 newscasts from the USA Radio News Network and Associated Press.
