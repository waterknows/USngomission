Boy Scouts of America, Grand Teton Council is the sponsoring community organization of 14 counties of southeastern Idaho and 2 counties of Western Wyoming and the city of West Yellowstone, Montana. Currently 700 Community Organizations who sponsor 1703 Units -- Cub Scout Packs, Scout Troops, Varsity Scout Teams and Venturing Crews who serve over 24,600 Youth under the leadership of more than 9,000 Volunteer Leaders.
