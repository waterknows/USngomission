Our mission: A worldwide charitable fellowship united by a common loyalty to Jesus Christ for the purpose of helping people grow in spirit, mind and body. For more than 140 years, the YMCA of Middle Tennessee has been giving people of all ages the tools they need to learn, grow and thrive. With 17 centers and hundreds of program locations, the YMCA reaches 311,385 people--nearly a third of them children--in the 8-county area it serves-by nurturing the potential of children and teens, improving the community's health and well-being and providing opportunities to serve others and support our neighbors.
