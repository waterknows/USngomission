Cornell College is a nationally acclaimed private liberal arts college in scenic Mount Vernon, Iowa. Founded in 1853, the college operates on a distinctive One-Course-At-A-Time academic structure that allows students to devote themselves to just one academic discipline during each of nine blocks. The college is committed to sustaining a community devoted to liberal learning and democratic values. The college offers more than 40 academic majors and pre-professional programs. Cornell's student body includes nearly 1,200 men and women from almost all 50 states and two dozen nations.
