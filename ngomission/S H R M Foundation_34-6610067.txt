The mission of the SHRM Foundation is to advance and develop higher standards of performance in the field of human resource management through research and development projects, to foster and encourage research, to establish scholarships, fellowships and similar educational aids, and to make grants to individuals or institutions involved with human resource management research or development activities. At the SHRM Foundation, we are a catalyst for thought leadership. We help predict where the workforce is headed because we've been studying its evolution for over 40 years. We offer unmatched workforce knowledge for progressive leaders, with a total focus on studying and reporting the management practices that work.
