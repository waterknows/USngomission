United Way of Broward County is a volunteer driven, community-based, nonprofit organization with a mission to improve the lives of individuals and families in Broward County. United Way of Broward County unites the resources of donors, volunteers and agencies to identify and target the community's most pressing needs and create measurable results that have the greatest impact. United Way of Broward County is dedicated to positively impacting the lives of people within our community. The core of United Way of Broward County's Community Impact model is to improve lives by mobilizing people to create real lasting changes in community conditions. To this end, we invest in programs that enhance the education, health and income of our community.
