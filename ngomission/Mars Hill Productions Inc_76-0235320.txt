Mars Hill Productions is a non-denominational ministry using media to draw people to Jesus Christ. For more than 30 years, Mars Hill Productions has produced award-winning films and videos that have been used around the world to introduce thousands of people to Jesus Christ, and to motivate thousands more to share their faith in Christ with others. Mars Hill began in 1977 as a division of Youth for Christ/Houston and was spun off as a separate ministry in 1988. Mars Hill has produced media projects for evangelism, apologetics, discipleship and mobilization in a variety of genres including dramatic film, documentary and video curriculum. The most recent Mars Hill project, The HOPE, is an 80 minute dramatic presentation of God's redemptive story from creation through Christ.
