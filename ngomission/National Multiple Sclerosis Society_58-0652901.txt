The mission of the National Multiple Sclerosis Society is to mobilize people and resources to drive research for a cure and to address the challenges of everyone affected by MS. The Georgia Chapter of the National MS Society is committed to assisting the thousands of Georgians impacted by MS continue moving their lives forward. The chapter raises funds locally to support the Society's critical research initiatives and to provide many comprehensive support services and educational programs to people living with MS, their family and friends. The Georgia Chapter of the National MS Society was chartered in April 1953 and currently serves clients in 159 counties in Georgia.
