Community School, St. Louis is an independent elementary school serving children age three through grade six. It is conveniently located in Ladue, nestled on 16 wooded acres with playgrounds, fields and nature trails. At Community School, children enjoy learning for the fun of it and secure the skills to excel - moving from wide-eyed curiosity to accomplished confidence. Community School's mission statement and educational philosophy flow from the ten educational principles upon which the School was founded in 1914. These principles have defined the ethos of the School for decades and the education of thousands of young children. Community School continues to believe in the educational values they represent.
