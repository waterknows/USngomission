Founded in 1981, the Atlas Economic Research Foundation, also known as the Atlas Network, works to strengthen the worldwide freedom movement by identifying, training and supporting individuals with the potential to found and develop effective independent organizations that promote our vision of a free, prosperous and peaceful world where limited governments defend the rule of law, private property and free markets. Today, the Atlas Network supports and works actively with 478 independent partner organizations that support the ideas of freedom in 83 countries.
