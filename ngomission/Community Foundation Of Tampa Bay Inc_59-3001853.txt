Founded in 1990, the Community Foundation of Tampa Bay works to build a better community through creative philanthropy, vision and leadership. The Community Foundation is a vehicle that supports new and existing programs of local nonprofit organizations in addition to its donor-supported Community Impact Fund. The Foundation is a trusted administrator of endowment funds and is supported by a broad spectrum of individuals, families, corporations, and private foundations. We connect donors who care with causes that matter. Currently with approximately $150 million in assets, the Community Foundation of Tampa Bay is one of the fastest growing new foundations in the country.
