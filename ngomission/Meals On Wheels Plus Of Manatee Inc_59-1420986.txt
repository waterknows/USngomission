The mission of Meals on Wheels Plus of Manatee, Inc. is to assist individuals to live independently by providing nutrition and caring supportive services. In 1972, Meals on Wheels of Manatee, Inc. was founded with the purpose of providing hot, nutritious meals to elderly and disabled residents of Manatee County. Over the years Meals on Wheels PLUS has responded with the necessary services to support the community's needs. Essential services that assist people to live independently include: nutrition, transportation, health monitoring, outreach, volunteer program, emergency aid assistance, and adult day care.
