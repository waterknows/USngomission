Plan International USA is part of a global organization that works side-by-side with communities in 50 developing countries to end the cycle of poverty for children. 



Plan develops solutions community by community to ensure long-term sustainability. Our level of community engagement, long-term outlook and constant focus on the needs and priorities of children is unique among international development organizations. Our solutions are designed up-front to be owned by the community for generations to come and range from clean water and healthcare programs to education projects and child protection initiatives.
