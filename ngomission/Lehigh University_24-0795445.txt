Founded in 1865, Lehigh University is a co-educational, non-denominational, private university located in Bethlehem, Pennsylvania. Lehigh enrolls 4,856 undergraduates and 2,118 graduate students, and has a student-to-faculty ratio of nine to one. Lehigh provides an education that lasts a lifetime. We blend the theoretical with the practical, and are proud of our ability to cross academic disciplines to provide an integrated learning experience. Our goal is to prepare students for not just their first job, but for their last one, as well.
