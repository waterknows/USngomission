Established in 1994, Bridge Builders International (BBI) brings people together to develop and strengthen leaders, build vital partnerships, and connect resources with needs and opportunities. Our vision is to see the transformation of Latvia by the love and power of the Lord Jesus Christ. We are committed to evangelical cooperation locally, regionally, nationally and internationally. Bridge Builders International is committed to the Biblical principle of the whole Church bringing the whole Gospel to the whole world. It wholeheartedly identifies with the purpose, theology, principles and passion of the international Lausanne Movement as expressed by the Lausanne Covenant.
