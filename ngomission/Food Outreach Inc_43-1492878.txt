Food Outreach is a St. Louis area organization whose mission is to provide nutritional support and enhance the quality of life of men, women and children living with HIV/AIDS or Cancer. It was founded in 1988 by friends caring for friends living with HIV/AIDS and then expanded to include those with cancer. In 2011, more than 484,333 meals were provided to clients in need. Food Outreach is run by 10 full-time employees, 3 part-time employees and over 600 volunteers who cook, pack and distribute meals.
