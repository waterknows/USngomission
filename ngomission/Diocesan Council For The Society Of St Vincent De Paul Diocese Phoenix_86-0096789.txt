The Society of St. Vincent de Paul - Phoenix has been serving homeless and economically disadvantaged populations since 1946. Through home visits, shelter, food assistance, employment services, thrift stores and outreach programs, St. Vincent de Paul provides compassion and support to individuals in need, regardless of race, origin, religion or gender. St. Vincent de Paul provides more than 3,500 meals daily to homeless and hungry people at its five Valley dining rooms and at 18 additional nonprofits. SVdP volunteers also provide more than 222,000 food boxes to working poor families and individuals throughout northern and central Arizona each year.
