Established in 1919, John Brown University (JBU) is a private, Christian university with more than 2,000 students from all over the U.S. and around the world. JBU offers nearly 50 undergraduate degree programs, three degree completion programs and seven graduate degree programs. JBU provides Christ-centered education that prepares people to honor God and serve others by developing their intellectual, spiritual, and professional lives. JBU is dedicated to one thing, educating the whole person - head, heart and hand. JBU is a community of believers, all working together to develop the talents that God has given each of us to further His kingdom.
