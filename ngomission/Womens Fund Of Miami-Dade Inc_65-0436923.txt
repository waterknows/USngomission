Women's Fund of Miami-Dade empowers women and girls through advocacy and funding for innovative initiatives that build equality, foster social change and create community partners. Founded in 1993, Women's Fund has awarded over $3 million to a wide range of innovative programs that provide women and girls the skills and resources necessary to improve their lives. By filling the enormous funding gap that exists for programs that exclusively address the unmet needs of women and girls, Women's Fund of Miami-Dade is celebrating 20 years of solutions. The Fund's website is www.womensfundmiami.org.
