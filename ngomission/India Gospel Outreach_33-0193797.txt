India Gospel Outreach's is a partnership of Indian and Western Christians facilitating mobilization, training, sending and supporting Indian evangelists and church planters to reach all of India for Jesus Christ. India Gospel Outreach starts new Bible training centers; aids poor people of India with relief, education and development; enlists intercessors to pray for spiritual awakening in India. India Gospel Outreach is dedicated to planting a dynamic church in each of India's 3,000 ethnic groups and 28,000+ zip codes.
