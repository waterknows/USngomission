Good360 is a nonprofit organization that is dedicated to fulfilling the needs of nonprofits by distributing corporate product donations around the world. The organization works with the nation's top retailers and brands to provide needed products to more than 22,000 qualified nonprofit organizations. This year, Good360 will launch the latest version of its new, innovative online marketplace that helps to aggregate the product needs of nonprofits and provides timely information about how product donations are used for the greater good. Over the last 28 years, Good360 has delivered more than $7 billion of donated products to thousands of nonprofit organizations, serving a wide spectrum of people in need.
