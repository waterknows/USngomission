Palo Alto Partners in Education (PiE) is committed to educational excellence for every student in the Palo Alto Unified School District (PAUSD). PiE works in partnership with parents, PAUSD, and the community to raise funds for an education beyond what is possible with public dollars. They aim to strengthen students' K-12 experience by supporting staff and innovative projects. PiE wholly focuses on fundraising to enhance educational excellence and is the embodiment of the community's value for education; the foundation represents a commitment to youth and an excellent public education.
