The Rescue Mission Alliance is a Christian organization ministering to the needs of the less fortunate in Southern California through emergency and long-term rehabilitation services. The Rescue Mission Alliance has served the homeless for more than 37 years. It has grown from the first location on Sixth Street in Oxnard to multiple locations in four counties throughout Southern California. Several distinct programs are offered at multiple locations, which provide a total Continuum of Care for addressing the needs of the hungry, homeless, and destitute.
