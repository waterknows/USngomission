WAMC/Northeast Public Radio is a regional public radio network serving parts of seven northeastern states. During the past year, WAMC provided public radio programming each month to over 300,000 residents of New York, Massachusetts, Connecticut, Vermont, New Jersey, New Hampshire and Pennsylvania. This programming includes news and cultural programming 24 hours a day, every day of the year. WAMC operates nine non-commercial educational FM radio stations, many FM translator stations, and several websites that distribute audio and visual materials. WAMC also distributes several programs produced at the organization's Albany studio to public radio stations across the nation and internationally.
