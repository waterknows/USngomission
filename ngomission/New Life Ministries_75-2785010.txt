New Life Ministries identifies and compassionately responds to the needs of those seeking healing and restoration through God's truth. Stephen Arterburn is the founder and chairman of New Life Ministries - the nation's largest faith-based broadcast, counseling and treatment ministry - and is the host of the nationally syndicated New Life Live! daily radio program heard on over 180 radio stations nationwide. New Life ministries provides Christian based self help resources in the form of books, tapes, videos, internet information and counseling resources.
