Camp Fire USA is a national youth serving organization serving more than 250,000 youth and their families annually. Camp Fire USA provides programs and opportunities for America's youth to prepare for life. 



Founded in 1910, Camp Fire USA's outcome-based programs remain at the forefront of youth development nationally. Our programs empower participants to build lives of healthy choices, lives connected to others, lives connected to the outdoors, and lives embracing good citizenship. Camp Fire programs also have family engagement components, which build further bonds through generations.
