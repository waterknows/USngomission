As a chapter of the national foundation, Make-A-Wish Orange County and the Inland Empire fulfills wishes for children between the ages of 2 1/2 and 18 who confront life-threatening medical conditions. The mission of Make-A-Wish Foundation is to enrich the human experience with hope, strength, and joy. Granting wishes helps these children find the hope they need to transform their life-threatening illnesses into life-affirming triumphs. Formed in 1983, the chapter has fulfilled the wishes of more than 5,000 children.
