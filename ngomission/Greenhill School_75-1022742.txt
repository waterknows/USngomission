Greenhill School is a diverse community of learners that strives for excellence; values individuality; fosters a passion for learning; promotes the balanced development of mind, body, and character; encourages service; and instills a respect for others. Greenhill was founded in 1950 as the first coeducational, nondenominational, PreK-12 school in Dallas. Our rich history is defined by excellence and achievement in a balanced program of academics, arts, athletics and community service which prepares our students to thrive in a changing world. An inclusive environment that celebrates and supports all voices, Greenhill is a community committed to the core principles of honor, respect and compassion. Currently, our 1,244 students come from families who reside throughout the Metroplex.
