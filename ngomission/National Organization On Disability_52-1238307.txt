The National Organization on Disability (NOD) is a private, non-profit organization that seeks to increase employment opportunities for the 79 percent of working age Americans with disabilities who are not employed. To achieve this goal, NOD offers a suite of employment solutions, tailored to meet leading companies' workforce needs. NOD has helped some of the world's most recognized brands be more competitive in today's global economy by building or enriching their disability inclusion programs. By partnering with employers and vocational rehabilitation centers, NOD helps employers to better recruit, hire, and retain individuals with disabilities by matching their abilities to jobs, then providing employer and employee with the support and training needed to ensure ongoing success. In parallel, NOD seeks to amplify our impact by widely promoting employment models that work.For more information about NOD, visit www.NOD.org.
