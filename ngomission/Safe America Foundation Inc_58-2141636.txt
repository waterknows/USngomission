The Safe America Foundation, chartered in 1994, with headquarters in Marietta, Georgia, partners with corporate, governmental, public and private sector organizations, and other nonprofits to improve the safety awareness and preparedness of Americans nationwide. We distribute information, develop and facilitate programming, and support businesses and products with a focus on emerging health and safety issues at home, work and play. We partner with civic organizations, government and corporations combining collective knowledge and skills to address emerging health and safety issues to promote a Safer America.
