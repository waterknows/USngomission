Founded in 1901, the Yale-China Association contributes to the development of education in and about China and to the furtherance of understanding and knowledge between Chinese and American people. Yale-China's work is characterized by sustained, long-term relationships designed to build Chinese institutional capacity. The Yale-China Association (雅礼协会) inspires people to learn and serve together. We foster long-term relationships that improve education, health, and cultural understanding in China and the United States
