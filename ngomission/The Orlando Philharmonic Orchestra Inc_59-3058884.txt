The mission of the Orlando Philharmonic Orchestra is to foster and promote symphonic music through excellence in performance, education and cultural leadership. The Orlando Philharmonic Orchestra is Central Florida's resident professional orchestra, appearing in more than 125 performances each season. The flagship series of the Orlando Philharmonic is the Super Series. This series is comprised of ten concerts, featuring the best of classics and pops, and includes five matinee performances to offer patrons even more entertainment options.
