The mission of Acterra is to bring people together to create local solutions for a healthy planet.Acterra is a regional environmental non-profit that has served Silicon Valley and the Bay Area since 1970. The organization provides people with tangible, hands-on activities to improve the environment. Acterra currently offers four programs providing thousands of adult and youth volunteer opportunities each year. Programs include: Stewardship Program, Energy Program, Business Environmental Awards, and the Environmental Library and Resource Center.Acterra was formed in 2000 by the merger of the Peninsula Conservation Center Foundation (founded 1970) and Bay Area Action (1990).
