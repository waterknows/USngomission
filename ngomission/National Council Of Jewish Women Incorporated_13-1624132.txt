National Council of Jewish Women New York Section is a grassroots organization of volunteers and advocates who turn progressive ideals into action. Inspired by Jewish values, NCJW New York Section strives for social justice by improving the quality of life for women, children and families and by safeguarding individual rights and freedoms. Through community service, education, and advocacy, NCJW New York Section improves the quality of life for families, women, children, and the elderly.
