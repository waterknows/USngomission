United Way of Smith County is working to strengthen our community in partnership with the agencies we support. Our goal is to create long lasting changes that prevent problems from happening in the first place. Our Nonprofit Development Center strives to build healthy nonprofits through its leadership training, coaching, and mentoring nonprofit leaders. You will find a wealth of resources in the funding research library. Our mission is to care for our community by partnering with you. Together, united we can inspire hope, and create opportunities for a better tomorrow.
