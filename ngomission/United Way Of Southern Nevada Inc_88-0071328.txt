MissionUnited Way of Southern Nevada works to advance the common good through our focus on education, health and financial stability, the building blocks of a good life. We recruit people and organizations from all around the community who bring the passion, expertise and resources needed to get things done. Together, person by person, we can make lasting change.VisionWe believe southern Nevada is a community where all people can achieve their human potential through a solid educational foundation, financial stability and healthy lives.
