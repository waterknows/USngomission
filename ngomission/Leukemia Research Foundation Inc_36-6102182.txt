Founded in 1946, the Leukemia Research Foundation (LRF) is dedicated to conquering all blood cancers by funding research into their causes and cures and enriching the life of those touched by these diseases. The LRF has more than 1,500 volunteers organized in 24 fundraising chapters. We provide patients with educational resources, emotional support, and financial assistance; additionally we fund research throughout the world.The LRF has relentlessly dedicated itself to finding a cure for more than 70 years.
