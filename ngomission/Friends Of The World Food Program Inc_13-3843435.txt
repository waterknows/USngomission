Established in 1995, World Food Program USA is a U.S.-based organization 501(c)3 that is dedicated to building support for the UN World Food Programme (WFP). WFP is the world's largest humanitarian agency fighting hunger worldwide, reaching more than 97 million people in 80 countries with food assistance last year. WFP USA increases awareness about global hunger issues; mobilizes support for hunger relief programs and activities; and generates resources for WFP operations and other activities that alleviate hunger. In collaboration with other groups, WFP USA is also active in building political support for U.S. government food aid programs.
