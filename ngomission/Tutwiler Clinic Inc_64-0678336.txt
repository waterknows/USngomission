Founded in 1983, the Tutwiler Clinic is a rural health clinic located in Tutwiler, Mississippi. It provides medical, dental, and optometry services to the community and surrounding areas. The clinic also provides ancillary health care and education to meet community needs on an outpatient basis. The Tutwiler Clinic's goal is to empower patients for health; whether it is by caring for Clinic patients, making house calls, treating hospitalized patients or nursing home residents.
