The Spectrum Health Foundation is dedicated to meeting donor's wishes to make a difference, to touch lives, and to belong to an organization that provides care and comfort to thousand of patients and families each year. Spectrum Health is the largest not-for-profit health care system in West Michigan. With seven hospitals and a multitude of prevention, diagnosis, treatment and recovery services, we are your partner for a better, healthier life. The Foundation partially funds the Fred and Leana Meijer Heart Center; funds the community initiative to promote the health of African American mothers and young children; supports a clinical oncology research study; and supports the poison center and programs.
