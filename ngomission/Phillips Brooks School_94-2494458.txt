Founded in 1978, the Phillips Brooks School (PBS) is an independent, coeducational day school enrolling 276 students in preschool through fifth grade. PBS prepares each student to live a creative, humane and compassionate life and to be a contributing member of society. PBS believes in blending the academic, physical and social aspects of its program with spiritual awareness and understanding. This balance provides an environment that celebrates individuality, promotes problem solving through critical thinking, and encourages students to fulfill their potential. PBS is dedicated to the creation of an environment in which each child can become a confident, respectful and compassionate individual by accepting and valuing the uniqueness of self and others.
