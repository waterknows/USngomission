Since 1947, the National Multiple Sclerosis Society, Southern California & Nevada Chapter has provided information and assistance to 19,000 people with multiple sclerosis and their loved ones. We reach out to more than 114,000 people touched by MS in Los Angeles, Riverside, San Bernardino, Inyo, Kern, Ventura, Santa Barbara, San Luis Obispo, Fresno, Kings, Madera and Tulare counties, as well as the whole state of Nevada. Offering much needed services and funding top-rated research, our dedicated staff members and volunteers are dedicated to achieving a world free of MS.
