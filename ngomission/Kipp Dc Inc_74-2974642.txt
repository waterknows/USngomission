KIPP DC is a network of high-performing, public, college-preparatory charter schools in Washington, D.C., which serves the city's under-resourced communities. At KIPP DC, there are no shortcuts. Outstanding educators, more time in school, a rigorous college-preparatory curriculum, and a strong culture of achievement and support help our students make significant academic gains and continue to excel in high school and college. Currently, KIPP DC reaches over 3,600 students at twelve schools on four campuses in D.C.'s Anacostia, Marshall Heights, Trinidad and Shaw neighborhoods.
