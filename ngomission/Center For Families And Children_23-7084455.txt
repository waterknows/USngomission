Center for Families and Children (CFC) makes success possible for people in Northeast Ohio's most challenged communities through exceptional direct client services in early learning, youth development and behavioral health, with a particular focus on providing access to options and opportunities for parents and adults living in poverty. Programs provided by CFC to those living in the greater Cleveland area include: Fathers and Families Together (FAFT), which helps equip fathers with the tools to become more responsible dads; the RapArt youth programs which work to promote positive character development; Behavioral Health, comprehensive mental health services; five child care centers; a government affairs and advocacy program; and Ease@Work, which provides quality employee assistance programs to more than 90 organizations throughout Northeast Ohio.
