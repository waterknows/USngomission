Founded in 1979, Jewish Family Services of Middlesex County is a nonprofit organization serving everyone who lives in Middlesex County and the surrounding environs. Jewish Family Services is committed to helping individuals and families realize a fulfilling life experience at home, in the workplace, and in the community by providing effective social services, counseling, and advocacy through responsive, innovative programs. The agency brings to the community a variety of hands-on, innovative solutions administered by skilled professionals, with the support of a dedicated Board of Trustees and a cadre of energetic volunteers. Jewish Family Services is a full service agency, which annually helps over 20,000 members of our community address problems that may be too complex or overwhelming to deal with alone.
