The mission of the National Multiple Sclerosis Society, Greater New England Chapter is to mobilize people and resources to drive research for a cure and to address the challenges of everyone affected by MS. The mission is supported by five strategies: research, advocacy, programs, awareness, and clinical. For nearly 60 years, the Greater New England Chapter has been providing help for today, and hope for tomorrow to nearly 16,000 individuals and families affected by multiple sclerosis in Vermont, New Hampshire and Massachusetts.
