KQED, also known as North California Public Broadcasting (NCPB), provides consistently high quality public media that informs, educates, entertains and engages from a Northern California perspective. Through the creation and acquisition of programs, the leveraging of our multiple media assets, and strategic partnerships, NCPB delivers television, radio, internet and education network content that makes people think, feel and explore new ideas. NCPB programs and services include KQED Public Television 9, one of the nation's most-watched public television stations during primetime, KQED Public Radio, a radio station with an award-winning news and public affairs program service, KQED Interactive, and the KQED Education Network.
