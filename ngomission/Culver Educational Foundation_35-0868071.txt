The Culver Academies comprise Culver Military Academy, Culver Girls Academy, and Culver Summer Schools and Camps. Culver Military Academy and Culver Girls Academy are independent college preparatory boarding schools that were founded in 1894 and 1971, respectively, while the six-week Culver Summer Schools and Camps program was established in 1902. Culver educates its students for leadership and responsible citizenship in society by developing and nurturing the whole individual - mind, spirit, and body - through an integrated curriculum that emphasizes the cultivation of character.
