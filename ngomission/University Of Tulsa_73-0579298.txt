Founded in 1894, the University of Tulsa (TU) is a private, independent, doctoral-degree-granting institution whose mission reflects these core values: excellence in scholarship, dedication to free inquiry, integrity of character, and commitment to humanity. TU educates men and women of diverse backgrounds and cultures to become literate in the sciences, humanities, and arts; think critically; succeed in their professions and careers; behave ethically in all aspects of their lives; welcome the responsibility of citizenship and service in a changing world; and acquire the skills and appetite for lifelong learning. University of Tulsa has an enrollment of over 4,000 undergraduate and graduate students.
