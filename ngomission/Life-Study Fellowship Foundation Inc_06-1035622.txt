Founded in 1939, Life-Study Fellowship's mission is to encourage people everywhere to lead a more prayerful and positive way of life. Life-Study Fellowship, publisher of FAITH Magazine, inspiring Fellowship Prayers, and the best selling book With God All Things Are Possible, has helped people all around the world find greater success, happiness and peace of mind. FAITH Magazine has now been published for almost 70 years. Published by Life-Study Fellowship bi-monthly, FAITH Magazine provides an everyday source of inspiration, guidance and comfort. By producing and publishing beautiful and inspiring Fellowship Prayers, and sending them to people in need, Life-Study Fellowship has helped countless numbers of people.
