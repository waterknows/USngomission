Teen Challenge of the Midlands provides hope in Christ, help for hurting people, and freedom from addiction. Teen Challenge of the Midlands reaches out to those with life controlling addictions and cares for them in their residential centers. In addition, local churches are assisted, regardless of affiliation, in establishing and operating support ministries for hurting people and families in their congregations and communities. Teen Challenge of the Midlands also encourages the general society by delivering first-hand presentations of lives that have been changed. Teen Challenge of the Midlands is one of over 150 Teen Challenge centers nationwide and over 100 centers in 60 countries. The first center opened in New York in 1960.
