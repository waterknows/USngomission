United Way of Whatcom County has worked for over 50 years to increase financial resources for local health and human service needs, and to seek and invest in community-building opportunities to improve Education, Income and Health for Whatcom County residents. Our mission is to increase the capacity to care for one another in Whatcom County and our 40+ funded programs touch more than 150,000 people each year. We focus on community wide goals for change in order to make a tremendous impact and by encouraging our community to Give, Advocate and Volunteer we mobilize workplaces, organizations and individuals to LIVE UNITED and make a better community for all.
