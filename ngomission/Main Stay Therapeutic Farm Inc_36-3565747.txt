Main Stay provides therapeutic riding, equine and animal assisted therapeutic activities for individuals with physical, developmental, emotional and social disabilities. Activities at the farm enrich lives in a caring, safe, and challenging environment that enables individuals to reach their full potential. Main Stay serves individuals who are faced with a wide variety of challenges. Carefully selected animals, large and small, are truly “rescued animals rescuing kids.” Main Stay is a place where opportunities are provided for at-risk, behaviorally, emotionally, developmentally, and physically challenged individuals.
