Darkness to Light's mission is to empower people to prevent child sexual abuse. By teaching adults to prevent, recognize and react responsibly to child sexual abuse through educational programs and public awareness of the issue, Darkness to Light addresses the root causes of child sexual abuse. With programs are in all 50 states and 16 additional countries, Darkness to Light is the largest and most well-known child sexual abuse prevention organization in the nation. In fact, it was recently listed as one of the "Top 50 national organizations to be thankful for" in Nonprofit Tech for Good, and is widely respected by professionals in the fields of pediatric medicine and social work.
