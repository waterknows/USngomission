Habitat for Humanity Seattle-King County is about much more than building houses. It's about building affordable housing, strong neighborhoods, responsibility and pride. Neighborhood revitalization is our primary goal, one that unifies everything we do. We believe that creating a healthy, vital neighborhood with affordable housing, and sustainable housing, can only happen when volunteers and neighbors come together to build a community.Habitat for Humanity Seattle-King County builds new communities here in Western Washington and around the world through our commitment to global engagement. Habitat for Humanity is more than an organization that builds houses. We are a movement that brings people together to build homes, communities and hope.
