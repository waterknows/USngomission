Catholic Charities of St. Paul and Minneapolis works to strengthen families and reduce poverty in the Twin Cities. The mission of Catholic Charities is to serve those most in need. We help individuals and families reach their full human potential as we call for justice in the community. With programs for children, families and adults experiencing homelessness, Catholic Charities helps 40,000 people annually, regardless of faith. Catholic Charities is a member of Catholic Charities USA, one of the largest private networks of social services providers in the country. Some of Catholic Charities' programs and services include: children's services, housing and emergency services, family services, and advocacy.
