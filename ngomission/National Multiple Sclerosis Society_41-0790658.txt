The National Multiple Sclerosis Society, Upper Midwest Chapter represents 17,000 people with MS in Iowa, Minnesota, North Dakota and South Dakota, as well as several counties in western Wisconsin and Nebraska. The National MS Society is a collective of passionate individuals who want to do something about MS now - to move together toward a world free of multiple sclerosis. We help each person address the challenges of living with MS through a 50-state network of chapters. The society helps people affected by MS by funding cutting-edge research, driving change through advocacy, facilitating professional education, and providing programs and services that help people with MS and their families move their lives forward.
