Established in 1983, Sonoma Country Day School (SCDS) is an independent coed prep school with an enrollment of 251 students, for grades K-8. SCDS draws students from Sonoma and Napa Counties and sends graduates to top national and local public and private schools. Sonoma Country Day School creates the environment to teach and model integrity in personal behavior, tolerance and respect in interpersonal relations, engagement in learning, and the pursuit of each child's capacity for excellence. SCDS is committed to providing students with a rigorous and exciting academic program, opportunities to develop their artistic and creative natures, ideals to open their potential for leadership, and self confidence to express their uniqueness.
