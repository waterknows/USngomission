Founded in 1805, PAFA is the birthplace of the American art museum and American art education, and has provided leadership in the arts and support for contemporary artists for over 200 years. The public programs offered by PAFA's museum harness its collection and its community of practicing artists to provide meaningful art education experiences for audiences of all ages, genders, ethnicities, and levels of expertise, and its School of Fine Arts trains the next generation of American artists. PAFA's institutional attendance reaches nearly 220,000 annually, including visitors from the Philadelphia area, across the country, and around the world.
