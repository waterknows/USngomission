HiTOPS is a nonprofit 501(c)(3) with the mission to promote adolescent health and well-being. HiTOPS envisions a world in which youth have the resources they need to live healthy and responsible lives. HiTOPS implements a model of education, health services and support designed to reduce risk behaviors and promote positive health outcomes. HiTOPS targets adolescents with developmentally appropriate health education to middle and high school students, high risk youth, parents and educators across NJ and within the only licensed ambulatory care facility exclusively for adolescents in New Jersey.At HiTOPS, we view youth as assets, not as problems.
