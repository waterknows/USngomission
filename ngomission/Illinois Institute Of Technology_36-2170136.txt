Illinois Institute of Technology (IIT) is private, Ph.D. granting research university founded in 1890. IIT is a leader in education that focuses on technology and engineering, and the application of those disciplines in multiple areas; offering programs in engineering, science, architecture, psychology, design, industrial technology, technical communication, business and law. IIT's mission is to advance knowledge through research and scholarship, to cultivate invention improving the human condition, and to educate students from throughout the world for a life of professional achievement, service to society, and individual fulfillment.
