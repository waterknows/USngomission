The Amyotrophic Lateral Sclerosis Association of Georgia's (ALSA of GA) mission is to lead the fight to cure and treat ALS through global, cutting-edge research, and to empower people with Lou Gehrig's Disease and their families to live fuller lives by providing them with compassionate care and support. Our vision is to be the preeminent organization and catalyst in directing, funding and promoting ALS research; most comprehensive provider of care and support services to the ALS Community through our coordinated network of Chapters, Centers and clinics; leading advocate and voice for the ALS Community; and to be the recognized authority and most trusted source of information and education about ALS.
