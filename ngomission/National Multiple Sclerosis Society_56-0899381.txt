The National Multiple Sclerosis Society, Greater Carolinas Chapter works to end the devastating effects of multiple sclerosis (MS). Founded in 1953, the National Multiple Sclerosis Society supports more MS research, offers more services for people with MS, provides more professional education programs and furthers more MS advocacy efforts than any other organization in the world. Our chapter programs team is nationally recognized for excellence in programs and advocacy, with services including: information and referral assistance; educational programs; direct services; outreach and education; wellness programs; and an advocacy program. On January 1st, 2012, the Mid Atlantic Chapter and the Eastern North Carolina Chapter merged to form the Greater Carolinas Chapter.
